What is DenVault?

Dentvault is your SUPERCHARGED digital file solution for all your digital dental needs.

Securely store, share, and access your dental data.

Share - DentVault allows you to send files up to 2GB per file. Receive an email / sms confirmation when the file you shared is viewed.

Access - All your files can be accessed from any computer or mobile device.

Secure - DentVault is HIPAA compliant and your files are stored using secure servers and are backed up in mirrored remote locations.

Collaborate - Share project notes with dentists who are sharing the file.

View - No need to download the files for viewing. Most file types can be viewed online directly in your vault.

Manage - Mark project status, organize by patient and much more.

 

How is DentVault different than other online storage services?

DentVault is designed for you, the dentist. DentVault is packed with features specific to dentistry in order to make it a helpful and valuable tool for you, your practice and your patients. With DentVault you can share case notes with associates, sort by patient, receive those large CT files, send files to your lab, view the files online and much much more.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Paid subscriptions can be cancelled at anytime. Prior to cancelling a paid subscription your account must meet the file requirements of the plan you would like to switch to. Files can be downloaded from your vault to your personal computer.

 

I am a dentist what can I use DentVault for?

Some of the most common ways dentists use DentVault are:

  • Receiving CT Scans from Scan Centers. Dentvault allows quick and efficient retrieval of dental CT Scans. No need to wait for a CD to mailed or the possibility that the CD is damaged or lost. Using DentVault dentists can download the CT scan for immediate use and keep a secure copy online for later use or to share with an associate.

  • Storing and accessing files. Dentists know that when their files are in their vault they can be accessed from any office, and any computer and any location.

  • Consultations. Files on Dentvault can be shared with an associate with the click of a button. Case notes can be added and viewed by all dentists viewing the file.

  • Sending files. Large files can be sent easily and quickly. Receive a confirmation when the receiver views the emails.
